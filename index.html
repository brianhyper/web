Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

What's included in your web development service?

Our web development service includes custom design, responsive development, basic SEO setup, content integration, and one round of revisions. We use modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and popular frameworks to ensure your site is fast, secure, and future-proof.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A basic website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 8-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation and project scoping.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer various maintenance plans to keep your website updated, secure, and performing optimally. Our plans include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support.

What's your design process like?

Our design process begins with research and discovery to understand your brand and goals. We then create wireframes and prototypes for feedback before moving to high-fidelity designs. Throughout the process, we collaborate closely with you to ensure the final product meets your expectations.

Can you help with content creation?

Absolutely! We have copywriters on our team who can create compelling content for your website, marketing materials, and social media. We can also coordinate with your existing content team if you prefer.
